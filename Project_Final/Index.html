Frequently Asked Questions

~Can I ask for a table with a view?

You can ask for a table with a view and your request will be noted. However, we cannot guarantee it as the front-of-house manager decides the layout of the tables each day, based on the number of reservations.

~At what times can we have lunch and dinner?

Lunch is served from 12:30-15:30. Dinner is served from 20:00-22:30.

~Can I order your products online?

You can order our products online. Contact through our hotline number for details.

~Can I choose a programme without a meal and add a lunch or dinner?

You can add a meal, lunch or dinner if you want. We have a Seasonal Menu and La Perla Tasting Menu, with drinks included.
